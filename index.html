The History of Dogs

Our domestic dog is descended from wolves. Even the tiny lap dog called the chihuahua. Can you believe that?

There are many breeds of Dogs

Some breeds of dogs include:

  1. The chihuahua
  2. The Husky
  3. The Border Collie
  4. The Pyranese

Other dog breeds include:

  • The Poodle
  • The Labrador
  • The Bulldog
  • The German Shepherd
